Academic Programs

O-Level Program

Our comprehensive O-Level program covers a wide range of subjects designed to provide students with a solid foundation in academics.

Subjects Offered:

  • English Language
  • Mathematics
  • Sciences (Physics, Chemistry, Biology)
  • History
  • Geography
  • Civics
  • Literature
  • Religious Education

A-Level Program

Our A-Level program offers specialized education in two streams:

Science Stream

  • Physics
  • Chemistry
  • Biology
  • Mathematics

Arts Stream

  • English Literature
  • History
  • Geography
  • Civics
  • Economics

Teaching Methodology

We employ modern teaching methods including:

  • Interactive classroom sessions
  • Laboratory practicals
  • Project-based learning
  • Group discussions and presentations
  • Use of multimedia resources

Assessment

Students are assessed through:

  • Continuous assessment (homework and class work)
  • Mid-term and end-term examinations
  • Practical assessments
  • Project submissions

Our Academic Programs

SAVIOR SSS offers comprehensive O-Level and A-Level education in both Arts and Science streams, designed to prepare students for university and professional success.

O-Level Program

A 4-year program providing broad-based education across multiple subjects:

Core Subjects

  • English Language
  • Mathematics
  • Social Studies
  • Religious Education

Science Subjects

  • Physics
  • Chemistry
  • Biology

Electives

  • Geography
  • Computer Science
  • Business Studies
  • And More

A-Level Program

A 2-year advanced program with specialization in Arts or Science:

Arts Stream

For students interested in humanities, social sciences, and languages:

  • Literature in English
  • History
  • Geography
  • Economics
  • Mathematics
  • General Paper

Science Stream

For students pursuing STEM and professional fields:

  • Physics
  • Chemistry
  • Biology
  • Mathematics
  • General Paper

Teaching & Learning

  • Experienced and qualified teaching staff
  • Interactive and student-centered learning methods
  • Practical laboratory sessions
  • Regular assessments and feedback
  • Guidance and counseling support
  • Extra tuition for students needing support

Student Support

  • Academic counseling and career guidance
  • Remedial classes for struggling students
  • Enrichment programs for advanced learners
  • Library with extensive learning resources
  • ICT facilities for research and learning
  • Health and wellness services

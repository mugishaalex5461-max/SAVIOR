New Science Laboratory Opening

April 2026

We are excited to announce the opening of our state-of-the-art science laboratory featuring the latest equipment including microscopes, spectrophotometers, and other modern apparatus. This facility will greatly enhance our science education program and provide students with hands-on learning experiences.

Annual Sports Day 2026 - Great Success

April 2026

Our annual sports day was a tremendous success with students showcasing exceptional athletic prowess across various disciplines. Participants competed in track and field events, football, basketball, and volleyball. The event fostered school spirit and camaraderie among students and staff.

Merit-Based Scholarship Program Launched

April 2026

We are pleased to announce the launch of a new merit-based scholarship program for deserving students. Scholarships will be awarded based on academic performance, financial need, and community contributions. Applications for the next academic year are now open.

Congratulations to our students on their excellent performance in the Cambridge examinations. Our O-Level and A-Level students achieved high pass rates, with many students receiving distinctions. We are proud of their hard work and dedication to academic excellence.

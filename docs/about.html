About SAVIOR Secondary School

Our Mission

SAVIOR Secondary School is committed to nurturing academic excellence, character development, and leadership qualities in our students. We provide a holistic education that prepares students for success in their future endeavors.

Our History

Located in Kisoro District on the slopes of Mount Muhabura, SAVIOR Secondary School has been serving the community with quality education for many years. Our institution is known for its commitment to excellence and student development.

Our Facilities

  • Modern classrooms with multimedia facilities
  • Well-equipped science laboratories
  • Extensive library with digital resources
  • Sports facilities including football and basketball courts
  • Comfortable boarding facilities for both male and female students
  • Dining hall and recreational facilities

Admissions Information

Admission Requirements

For O-Level Form 1

  • Must have completed Primary education
  • Minimum age of 13 years
  • Good academic performance
  • Pass entrance examination

For A-Level Form 5

  • Must have completed O-Level (Senior 4)
  • Minimum of 40% in O-Level final examination
  • Good conduct record
  • Relevant subject prerequisites

Application Process

  1. Complete the application form with all required information
  2. Attach supporting documents (birth certificate, academic records)
  3. Pay the application fee (UGX 50,000)
  4. Submit to the Admissions Office
  5. Sit entrance examination (if applicable)
  6. Await notification of admission status

Fees

Tuition fees vary depending on whether students are day scholars or boarders:

Day Scholars (per term)

  • Tuition: UGX 500,000
  • Examination Fees: UGX 50,000
  • Activity Fees: UGX 30,000

Boarders (per term)

  • Tuition: UGX 750,000
  • Boarding: UGX 500,000
  • Examination Fees: UGX 50,000
  • Activity Fees: UGX 30,000

Note: Scholarships available for exceptional students

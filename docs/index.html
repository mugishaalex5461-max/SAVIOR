About SAVIOR SSS

Education Excellence in Western Uganda

SAVIOR Secondary School, located in Nyakinama, Bufumbira, Western Uganda, is a mixed day and boarding institution dedicated to providing quality education. Our Centre No. U3734 ME/32/5760 represents our commitment to excellence in O-Level and A-Level education across Arts and Science streams.
